PAC-MAN

Play Pac-Man
Pac-Man is an action maze chase video game in which the player controls the character in an enclosed maze. The objective of the game is to eat all of the dots placed in the maze while avoiding ghosts that pursue him.

Creator: Toru Iwatani
Original Release: 1980

Instructions

  1. Try to eat all the dots in the enclosed maze before getting caught by the ghosts.
  2. Use the Left Arrow Key, Right Arrow Key, Up Arrow Key, and Down Arrow Key to move Pac-Man in the respective direction.
  3. Small dots will award you with 1 point and bigger dots will award you with 10 points.
  4. Have Fun!
